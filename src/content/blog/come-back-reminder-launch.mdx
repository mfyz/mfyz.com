We're excited to announce that our first app is now live on the Shopify App Store! 

## What does it do?

Come Back Reminder helps you recover traffic and sales from users who were distracted away from your store. When a visitor is about to leave your site, our app helps bring them back to complete their purchase.

## Try it out!

You can install Come Back Reminder directly from the Shopify App Store.

Want to learn more about how Come Back Reminder can help your store? Check out our [product page](/apps/come-back-reminder) for detailed features and benefits.
